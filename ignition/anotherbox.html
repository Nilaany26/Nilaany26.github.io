We are a group of Grade 10 and 11 students from Canada and India who met through the Ignition Hackathon Program. We are passionate individuals in the field of Technology, Programming, and AI. We call ourselves Operation Sigma, derived from the division we participated in. We worked and collaborated together using the knowledge we know, and are always trying to boost our skills.
