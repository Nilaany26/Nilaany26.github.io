Operation Sigma has worked to make a scheduler and event organizer for students and learners to manage time and accomplish tasks within the due date. Our Vision is to help these people to have a better time learning and prioritizing time for School and Personal Work. We provide a website that can manage, modify and keep track of the work they need to do. This schedule is very easy to use and the best part, you do not need to sign up and it is completely free.
