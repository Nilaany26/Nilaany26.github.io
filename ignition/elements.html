The Idea Behind

The Idea Behind this website and chatbot is to help fellow students manage time and have a schedule that can be easily made and accessed.

How it is helpful?

The Chatbot allows students and learners to talk with it, and helps them organize themself in a systematic way. We have built this for 2 main reasons: 1) Reduce Stress and Pressure among students 2) Talk openly with someone who can listen(Mitra).

How to Manage Time?

You can manage time by Prioritizing your work and setting due dates. You have to use the strategy that works for you. There are various options to choose from. Some such examples include Pomodoro technique, which is a timer that allos you to stay focused and finsih your tasks. There are 3 key factors in managing time: Staying Focused/being Productive, Organizing tasks and times and planning for your work. With our schedule builder, you can easily plan your work and modify changes.
