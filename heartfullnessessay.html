Kindness-for self, for each other and the environment

Kindness - a word like a warm hug :

Kindness is a beautifully distinguished reason to call ourselves ‘Humane’. In this fast-forwarding world where we are living our lives with many constraints scattered here and there, Kindness is the key for us to move on with an optimistic mindset. A token of kindness is like a whisper from an angel who says,” you will be fine”.

Kindness-For self :

We have always craved to have a motivator in our lives to help us move forward in our journey. Most of us watch motivational videos, listen to podcasts but there is a most underrated motivator who has traveled with each one of us throughout our life. It’s none other than ourselves. Yes, we often forget to thank ourselves. It is highly essential that we pamper ourselves, once in a while to get us going. While we expect others to be kind to us, why not kickstart our journey by being kind to ourselves. Expressing kindness for oneself might not be in form of treating ourselves with a luxurious spa or an expensive meal, but a tiny thing like a warm smile in front of the mirror does wonders to our soul.

Kindness-For each other :

Newton’s 3rd law of motion is called the law of life. When we are wondering how is that correlating, It says that, “Every action has an equal and an opposite reaction”. This means when we are being kind to others, kindness finds its way to us. Kindness is never to be considered a deed of great difficulty. Even the simplest of matter of kindness has the potential to make someone’s day. The realization that everybody has their own set of hurdles in life and that the world is better when we are being kind to each other is the pinnacle to peace, says an infamous quote.

Kindness-For the environment:

Earth is seen as a powerful planet that has the perfect resources to host us comfortably with all the basic needs of survival. There is always a norm to give back in the act of being thankful. It is high time that we all find a way to give back to our mother Earth, Which is by being kind to the environment. Let us wake up and realize that our environment does have feelings and is already seeking our help. Let’s make tiny changes that make our life more environment friendly and communicate with the environment through the language of kindness.
